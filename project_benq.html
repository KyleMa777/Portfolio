Evaluating Zowie's E-Sports Booking Platform

Zowie is BenQ's e-sports arena booking platform, newly launched when I joined the team. I ran mixed-methods research to evaluate the booking experience and turned what I found into wireframes that shipped.

What this shows Mixed-methods research, cross-functional collaboration, wireframing
Context Internship · BenQ Corporation · Zowie E-Sports Platform
Timeline June – September 2023

In this project, I...

  • Ran mixed-methods user research, including survey design and interviews, to evaluate the newly launched Zowie e-sports arena booking platform
  • Turned research data into actionable design insights through reports, helping cross-functional teams align on the booking funnel's critical pain points
  • Designed high-fidelity wireframes and optimized navigation structures for the service booking flow
  • Contributed to a 15% increase in product sales by improving the efficiency of that flow
